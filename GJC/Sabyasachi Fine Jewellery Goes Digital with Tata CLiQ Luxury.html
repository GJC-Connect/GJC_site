Sabyasachi Fine Jewellery Goes Digital with Tata CLiQ Luxury

India’s love affair with luxury enters a new chapter as celebrated couturier-turned-fine jewellery designer Sabyasachi Mukherjee launches his first digital fine jewellery boutique in an exclusive partnership with Tata CLiQ Luxury, going live on 21st August 2025.

Team GJC • 2 hours ago • 4 min read
For the first time, discerning buyers across the country can explore and shop Sabyasachi’s signature fine jewellery online, crafted in 18-karat gold and set with VVS-VS EF colour brilliant-cut diamonds, natural pearls, and precious gemstones—a collection where heritage meets modern elegance.

The boutique will showcase the largest curation of Sabyasachi Fine Jewellery online, including iconic collections:

  • Royal Bengal Heritage Gold – featuring the Bengal Tiger insignia in pure gold, alongside the classic Sabyasachi Mangalsutra.
  • Royal Bengal Pearl – crafted with natural, cultured, and South Sea pearls.
  • Sunderbans Collection – inspired by flora and fauna, brought alive through ancient crafts.
  • Tiger Stripe & Shalimar – modern interpretations of the house insignia in 18-karat gold with lacquer accents.
  • Tiger Eye – highlighted by a diamond-studded Bengal Tiger motif.

Reflecting on the launch, Sabyasachi Mukherjee said:

“Some of the most superlative jewellery ever made in India has been handcrafted by the master goldsmiths of Calcutta, the ‘babus’. I want to make this legacy thrive again. This collection is grounded, not aspirational, yet remains everything you’d expect from the House of Sabyasachi: refined, rooted, timeless.”

Gopal Asthana, CEO, Tata CLiQ Luxury, added:

“This launch strengthens our vision of bringing the finest Indian and global luxury to discerning consumers, from metros to Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities. Sabyasachi’s heritage and craftsmanship redefine fine jewellery for the digital era.”

With this partnership, Tata CLiQ Luxury and Sabyasachi not only open doors to timeless jewellery but also reimagine the way India experiences luxury—elevated, accessible, and deeply rooted in heritage.
